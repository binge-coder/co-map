Outcome-Based Education

The future of education assessment â€” focused on measurable student outcomes and continuous improvement.

Outcome-Based Education (OBE) is a student-centered approach that emphasizes specific, measurable learning outcomes. Rather than focusing solely on what is taught, OBE prioritizes what students actually learn and can demonstrate after completing a course or program.

The core philosophy of OBE is that all educational activities should align with desired learning outcomes, ensuring students acquire necessary skills, knowledge, and attitudes by the end of their academic journey.
